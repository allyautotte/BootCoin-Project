About Us

Bootcoin was developed in September 2017 in San Diego, CA. This app was designed to help investors in the crypto-currency markets compare international exchange rates in order to help them make smart financial decisions. Bootcoin can help savvy investors bost their personal income by utilizing our unique interface to compare up-to-the-minute BTC and ETH rates.

We are a dedicated team of future full-stack developers intent on changing the world with new and exciting applications.
